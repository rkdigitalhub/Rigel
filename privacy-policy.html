Privacy Policy

This policy explains how Rigel Market collects, uses, stores, and protects personal information when you use our website and services.

1. Information We Collect

  • Identity and contact data, such as full name, email, phone number, and address.
  • Account and compliance data, including verification and regulatory information.
  • Technical usage data, such as device details, browser type, IP address, and activity logs.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain trading and account services.
  • To perform identity checks, fraud monitoring, and legal compliance.
  • To improve platform performance, support quality, and user experience.
  • To communicate account updates, operational notices, and support responses.

3. Legal Basis and Data Sharing

We process data based on contractual necessity, legal obligations, legitimate interests, and consent where required.

  • Data may be shared with regulated service providers for hosting, compliance, payments, and analytics.

4. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to support core site functionality, security monitoring, and analytics.

5. Retention and Security

  • Data is retained only as long as necessary for service, compliance, and legal recordkeeping purposes.
  • We apply administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards to protect personal information.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, restrict, or object to processing of your personal data.

To exercise privacy rights, contact us at support@rigelmarket.com.

7. Policy Updates

We may revise this privacy policy periodically. Updates will be posted on this page.
